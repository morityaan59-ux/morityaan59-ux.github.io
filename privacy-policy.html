Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how My TikTok Poster ("the App") handles your information.

1. Information We Collect

The App collects the following information solely for the purpose of posting videos to TikTok:

  • TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token (stored locally on your device)
  • TikTok user account ID
  • Video files and captions you choose to post

2. How We Use Your Information

All data is stored locally on your own computer. The App does not operate any external servers. Your TikTok credentials are used only to authenticate with the TikTok API and post content on your behalf.

3. Data Sharing

The App does not share your data with any third parties except TikTok (via the official TikTok API) as required to perform posting operations. No data is sold or shared for advertising purposes.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses the TikTok API. Please review TikTok's Privacy Policy for information on how TikTok handles your data.

5. Data Storage

All settings and tokens are stored in a local file (settings.json) on your device. You can delete this file at any time to remove all stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via GitHub.
